Thirty-Second Annual AI Conference Extended To Forty-Five Seconds

   The AAAI artificial intelligence conference announced this week that their thirty-second annual meeting would be extended by 50% to forty-five seconds.

   “We simply felt that thirty seconds would not be enough time to discuss all topics in their deserved depth,” AAAI President Subbarao Kambhampati told the Times.

   At press time, the extra 15 seconds was reportedly earmarked for increased human involvement.
